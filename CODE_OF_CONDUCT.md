# Code of conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We're committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone. We don't tolerate discriminatory or threatening behavior, and we take reports seriously. We expect contributors and maintainers to engage with civility, assume good faith, and stay focused on the work.

## Reporting

Email **conduct@spear.example**. Reports go to a small rotation of maintainers and are kept confidential. We respond within **5 business days** with next steps.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may, at their discretion: warn, request edits to public posts, mute participants in a thread, or — for repeated or severe violations — remove a contributor's access. Decisions can be appealed to the same address.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: this repository, its issues + PRs + Discussions, any chat or video calls associated with the project, and any in-person interactions where one or more participants are representing the project.
